# Publications, decrees, deliberations of the municipal council,…

Access here the decrees of the mayor, the prefecture, and the deliberations of the councils.

The municipal council meets several times a year. Its deliberations govern the affairs of the municipality. The meetings are open to the public. You can find these deliberations here.
